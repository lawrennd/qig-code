## Motivation

The breakthrough to the two-stage perspective (dephasing, isolation) with Fisher eigenvalue hierarchy fundamentally changes how the story flows from information axioms to emergent physics. The paper needs to:

1. *Separate axioms from dynamics* clearly
2. *Present the two-stage evolution* as the central narrative 
3. *Use Jaynes natural parameters* consistently throughout
4. *Show emergent gauge theory and particles* as consequences of Fisher geometry
